CASES

My first case! Exciting isn't it?

Let me introduce you ... Job Ahoi!

In subject Workplace Learning 2, we had the opportunity to participate in an exciting project, where we were outsourced by the company Salamander! The project revolved around developing
a job matching app for the metal, construction and logistics sectors, and creating a launch
website to present the benefits of the app.

The project's requirements were that the app should be fast, simple and user-friendly , and keep the concept approachable without the need for a CV. Use of the app is completely free
for candidates, they can browse completely anonymously and therefore only need to share
personal information during the application process.

Coding & Brandbook

Within the project, I had the responsibility of being part of the team as a front-end developer. In a group of 5, I worked with two other colleagues to build the launch website, where I was specifically responsible for creating the index page, implementing card elements, coding the navigation and footer, and applying responsiveness. And we created all of this with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

I also made 6 JavaScript codes : sign in/up, navigation bar that changes after signing in, an automated counter, body loading and modal that appears when you checkout.

The designs themselves were created by the other 2 colleagues. they chose blue as the primary color of trust and loyalty towards our customers.... and blue from the sea! We are pirates remember? After the branding was discussed, I created the brand guidelines with Illustrator.

Check out my final reflection (dutch) if you want to know more about my experience I have gained through this project, and what I have learned from it.
